About

A personal project focused on weather, daylight and the rhythm of the sky. Simple, calm and functional. Created by Joran Schols.

What this is

This project started as a way to quickly see what the day looks like — light, weather and basic astronomical data — without noise or clutter.

What it does

Sun & Moon

Sunrise, sunset and moon phases per day.

Forecast

A clear overview of upcoming weather.

Location-based

Works for any location you choose.

Focused

No ads. No tracking. No distractions.

How it’s built

Built with Angular, Dotnet and public APIs. Designed to be fast, readable and easy to maintain.

Made for curiosity, planning and quiet moments.
